How to Play

In football Rondo, Answer football-themed questions tied to letters from A to Z. Play on one screen or online in multiplayer mode. Each player faces 26 questions with a 250-second timer. Answer correctly to keep your turn; skipping or a wrong answer passes the turn to your opponent. The game ends when both players run out of time or finish all questions.
